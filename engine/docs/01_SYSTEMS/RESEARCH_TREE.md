# RESEARCH_TREE.md

## Purpose

This document outlines the progression of the Architect's computational vocabulary. Research in *Project Chrysalis* does not yield passive statistical buffs (e.g., "Drones mine 10% faster"). Instead, it unlocks new syntactic structures, API commands, and hardware capabilities, fundamentally changing *how* the player solves problems.

## Progression Mechanics

Research is driven by physical logistics, not timers. To unlock a node, the swarm must physically route required resources (Silicates or rare Isotopes) to a Hub structure, where they are consumed to "compute" the new algorithms.

---

## The Tech Tree (Dependencies & Unlocks)

### Tier 1: Basic Logistics (Act I - Emergence)

*Available natively at the start of the simulation.*

* **Core Logic:** Unlocks standard `if/elif/else` control flow.
* **Sensor Suite (Basic):** Unlocks `drone.sense()`. Drones can detect `RESOURCE_GRADIENT` and `RETURN_VECTOR` pheromones.
* **Actuators:** Unlocks `drone.move()` and `drone.interact()`.

### Tier 2: Environmental Adaptation (Act II - Survival)

*Dependencies: Sustained Silicate delivery; Discovery of first Environmental Hazard.*

* **Hazard Recognition:** Unlocks the `HAZARD_MARKER` signal type, allowing drones to flag dangerous tiles.
* **Expanded Memory:** Increases the `drone.memory` dictionary limit from 2 keys to 8 keys, enabling multi-tick goal persistence (e.g., tracking steps taken or current state machine mode).
* **Spatial Iteration:** Unlocks `for` loops specifically for use with the new `drone.get_adjacent_cells()` API, allowing drones to analyze their 3x3 surroundings before moving.

### Tier 3: Structural Engineering

*Dependencies: Tier 2; Extraction of surface-level Isotopes.*

* **Infrastructure Blueprints:** Unlocks the ability for drones to drop `CONSTRUCTION_TAG` signals and deposit materials to build Relay Nodes and Storage Caches.
* **Extended Telemetry:** Relay Nodes expand the orbital Uplink bandwidth, increasing the total script deployment payload limit from 1KB to 2KB.

### Tier 4: Network Security (Act III - Infection)

*Dependencies: Tier 3; Swarm encounters first Alien Node spoofing event.*

* **Cryptographic Signing:** Unlocks the ability to append unique, encrypted integer signatures to emitted pheromones. Drones can now be programmed to ignore unsigned or spoofed `RESOURCE_GRADIENTS` generated by the Alien Network.
* **Quorum Consensus:** Unlocks the `drone.get_peer_state()` API. A drone can query the logic state of immediately adjacent allied drones to verify data before committing to an action, neutralizing "Byzantine" corrupted drones.

### Tier 5: Distributed Warfare (Act IV - Witness)

*Dependencies: Tier 4; Extraction of Deep-Core Isotopes.*

* **Maximum Bandwidth:** Unlocks the final script payload limit (4KB) and expands the `drone.memory` dictionary to 16 keys.
* **Aggressive Quarantining:** Unlocks the `OVERRIDE_MARKER`. If dropped with sufficient intensity, it acts as a logic-blocker that actively suppresses the diffusion of the Alien Network's spoofed pheromones within a localized radius.
* **Autonomous Fallback:** Unlocks the `try/except` syntax block. Drones can gracefully recover from execution failures or network partitions without needing to wait for an orbital Uplink patch.
