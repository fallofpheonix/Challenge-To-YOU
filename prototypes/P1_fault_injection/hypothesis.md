# Hypothesis

Players enjoy a single controllable perturbation when it produces immediate, explainable consequences.

## Prototype

P1

## Research Question

Can one tiny intervention create enough curiosity that players voluntarily repeat experiments?

## Expected Outcome

Players will voluntarily perform additional experiments beyond what is required, because the interaction is immediately understandable, surprising, and explainable.

## Success Metrics

- Voluntary experiments: > 5 before solving
- Different approaches: at least 3 across testers
- Correct explanations: > 70% after solving
- "One more try" moments: at least one per tester
- Stories told afterward: > 50% of testers
- Time To First Voluntary Experiment (TTFVE): under 30 seconds
- Hypothesis Count: at least 2 explicit "what if" or "maybe" statements per tester
