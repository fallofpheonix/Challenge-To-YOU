# Challenge To YOU — Multiverse Bestiary & Security Systems

This document catalogs the hostile daemons, security constructs, biological mutations, and system anomalies that patrol the 14 universes.

## 1. Magical Security (Magitech & Void)

### 1.1 Leyline Siphon
* **Universe**: Medieval Magitech
* **Threat Level**: Low
* **Behavior**: Hovers over active rune channels, absorbing mana packets. 
* **Puzzle Effect**: Drains player mana state by 5 points per turn if the conduit remains unsealed.
* **Lore**: Centralized energy gates constructed by the Royal Assembly to detect unlicensed magic users.

### 1.2 Thread Leach
* **Universe**: Cosmic Void
* **Threat Level**: Medium
* **Behavior**: Attaches to active asynchronous routines, delaying callbacks.
* **Puzzle Effect**: Adds a 500ms delay to thread executions, increasing the chance of timeout.
* **Lore**: Void anomalies that feed on processing currents in asynchronous channels.
