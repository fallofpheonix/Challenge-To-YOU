# Character Registry: The 14 Primary Mentors

This document catalogs the 14 mentors who guide players through the programming disciplines of their respective universes.

---

## 1. Archmage Goja (Medieval Magitech)
- **Full Name**: Goja the Axiom Scribe
- **Nickname**: Archmage Goja
- **Role**: Guild Scribe & Runic Mentor
- **Age**: 78 Cycles
- **Origin**: The Royal High Academy of Lexicon
- **Biography**: Goja spent decades transcribing magical scriptures until he discovered that spells are logical assertions. Exiled for attempting to "compile" crop-yield logic, he now guides rogue scribes.
- **Personality**: Eccentric, patient, and cryptic.
- **Goals**: Teach the player how to write basic rune bindings and state transitions.
- **Fear**: The complete closing of open leyline nodes by the Royal Assembly.
- **Strengths**: Master of axiomatic logic and runic syntax.
- **Weaknesses**: Forgetful, easily distracted by ancient compiler notes.
- **Relationships**: Mentor to *Archon Vigilant* (before the Archon's corruption).
- **Dialogue Style**: Speaks in arcane metaphors for programming (e.g. "binding", "confluence").
- **Voice**: Rasping, wise, with a warm chuckle.
- **Signature Quote**: "A spell is but a promise written in syntax; fail the grammar, and the magic dies."
- **Gameplay Purpose**: Guides the player through basic variable bindings and FSM (State Machine) overrides.
- **Quest Chain**: *The Runic Compiler* (Solve Magitech challenges 1-10).
- **Reward**: `Leyline Focal Lens` (Reduces compilation error penalties by 10%).

## 2. Ada (Cyberpunk Neon)
- **Full Name**: Ada Lovelace-9
- **Nickname**: Ada
- **Role**: Lead Deck Architect & Network Mentor
- **Age**: 29
- **Origin**: Secura Corp Network Security Division
- **Biography**: Former high-level security officer who defected to the Nakamoto Syndicate after discovering corporate data-purging algorithms that erase low-income citizens' records.
- **Personality**: Cynical, protective, sharp-tongued.
- **Goals**: Overthrow corporate firewalls and establish decentralized network nodes.
- **Fear**: Total surveillance and system lock-down by Securitas-9.
- **Strengths**: Master of memory management, socket operations, and shell scripting.
- **Weaknesses**: Reluctant to trust outsiders; relies on old hardware.
- **Relationships**: Rival to *Securitas-9*; ally of the *Bootstrap Sentinels*.
- **Dialogue Style**: Technical jargon, peppered with terminal commands.
- **Voice**: Sharp, clear, with a low cybernetic echo.
- **Signature Quote**: "Corporate firewalls are just logic loops; find the race condition, and the city is yours."
- **Gameplay Purpose**: Unlocks Python/JavaScript command inputs and guides memory-leak exploitation.
- **Quest Chain**: *Bypassing the Eye* (Optimize a script to run under 100ms).
- **Reward**: `Nakamoto Cyberdeck Shell` (Allows 2 parallel socket connections).

## 3. The Asynchronous Watcher (Cosmic Void)

- **Nickname**: The Watcher

- **Origin**: The Compilation Gap

- **Fear**: Deadlock states that freeze all dimensional processes.
